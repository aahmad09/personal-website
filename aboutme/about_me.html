About me

My name is Asjal Ahmad, and I’m a student at Trinity University fueled by my passion for computer science. I’ve been coding since I was a child, building promo websites for small businesses near my school. As I kept on learning, I shifted my focus from simple CSS & HTML websites to web apps and multi-threaded games. After that, it has been a non-stop road of learning and delight. I have picked up many languages, platforms, and technologies along the way, each even more exciting than the last. At the end of the day, I consider myself a lifelong student, eager to learn the latest technologies while continuing to make people's lives better through the magic of programming.

Apart from being a software developer, I love traveling and volunteering for community service organizations. At times when I am coding by myself, I like to listen to classical piano like Beethoven and Bach. To blow off some steam, I usually play immersive story-driven video games.
